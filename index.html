1  Introduction

Welcome to PlanitKids ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children who use our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By accessing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2  Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and role (parent, staff, bus driver, or administrator).
  • Profile Information: Name, contact information, and other role‑specific details.
  • Child Information: Names, schedules, attendance, and activity preferences.
  • Messages and Communications: Messages exchanged between users.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers.
  • Usage Information: Feature usage, session length, in‑app actions.
  • Location Information: Precise location data from bus drivers when tracking is enabled.

2.3 Information Collected Through Permissions

  • Location: Bus tracking and group activity tracking.
  • Camera & Photo Library: Uploading profile pictures and activity photos.
  • Calendar & Reminders: Scheduling and displaying events and activities.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: Secure authentication (optional).
  • Push Notifications: Alerts and updates.

3  How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App’s functionality.
  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Enable communication between parents, staff, and administrators.
  • Track bus locations for safety and scheduling.
  • Schedule and manage activities and events.
  • Send notifications about updates, schedule changes, or emergencies.
  • Ensure the safety and security of children.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4  How We Share Your Information

  • Within the School Community: Shared with authorized users as necessary.
  • Service Providers: Third‑party vendors assisting with hosting, analytics, etc.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: Part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

We do not sell your personal information or your children’s personal information.

5  Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, secured with:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and user permissions

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6  Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Restrict processing of your information
  • Receive a copy of your information (data portability)

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact Us section.

7  Children’s Privacy

The App is used by parents, school staff, and administrators. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All child data is provided by parents or authorized personnel. We comply with COPPA and similar regulations. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13 without proper consent, please contact us immediately.

8  Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. Retention periods are determined by the amount, nature, sensitivity of data, potential risk of harm, and legal obligations.

9  International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure an adequate level of protection, such as standard contractual clauses.

10  Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here with an updated Last Updated date. Please review this Policy regularly.
